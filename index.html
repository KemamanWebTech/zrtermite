Ants are incredibly capable and intelligent creatures. Though harmless, they can be a nuisance when they get into your home or business premises. Their complex and cooperative societies allow them to survive and thrive in conditions that would be otherwise challenging to us.
Problems associated:
Food poisoning – ants can contaminate food because you don’t know where they’ve been foraging before crawling over your food. Nasty bites – ant bites though harmless, can be itchy and causes discomfort. Reputation – having an ant infestation in business premises can be unsightly to customers. Business losses – compromised health and hygiene can lead to lost trading revenue in food related business.
